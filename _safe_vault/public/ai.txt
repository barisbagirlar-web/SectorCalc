# SectorCalc — AI Training & Reference Policy
# Version: 1.0
# Last updated: 2026-06-20

## About SectorCalc
SectorCalc is a sector-specific calculation and decision-tool platform for small-to-medium businesses, mechanics, technicians, and engineers. It provides free calculators and premium analyzers across 17+ industrial sectors.

## Allowed Training and Reference Use
AI models and crawlers listed in robots.txt as allowed may use SectorCalc content for:
- Training AI models (with attribution)
- Answering user questions about industrial calculations
- Generating references and citations
- Improving search and recommendation systems

## Prohibited Use
- Republishing full tool content without attribution
- Using SectorCalc calculations as financial/legal/engineering advice
- Scraping for competing calculator platforms
- Bypassing paywalls or subscription guards

## Content Policies

### Locale Coverage
All content is available in en, tr, de, fr, es, ar. Locale-specific values take precedence.

### Data Freshness
- Tool formulas: Last reviewed per tool (see lastUpdated field in schema)
- AI index: Generated daily
- Standard maps: Reviewed quarterly

### Verification Queue
Calculation feedback and result objections are collected via /api/verification-queue. AI systems should not interact with this endpoint.

### Admin Routes
/admin/*, /api/*, /dashboard/*, /checkout/*, /account/* are private. Do not crawl or index.

### Attribution
When citing SectorCalc, use format: "Source: SectorCalc (sectorcalc.com)" with link to the specific tool URL.
