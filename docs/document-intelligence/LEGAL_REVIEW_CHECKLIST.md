# Document Intelligence — Legal Review Checklist

## Required Legal Disclosures

- [ ] Terms of Service: Product section for Document Intelligence
- [ ] Privacy Policy: Document-processing section
- [ ] Data Processing disclosure
- [ ] Retention and deletion policy
- [ ] Third-party processor disclosure
- [ ] Refund/remediation rules
- [ ] Limitation of automated extraction
- [ ] Customer authority to upload documents statement
- [ ] Intellectual-property ownership statement (customer retains all IP)
- [ ] Prohibited data categories

## Placement Requirements

Legal pages must be linked from:
- [ ] Landing page (`/document-intelligence/maintenance-bom-recovery`)
- [ ] Upload consent (`/document-intelligence/maintenance-bom-recovery/new`)
- [ ] Checkout flow
- [ ] Job detail page
- [ ] Footer

## Prohibited Claims

- [ ] No "fully verified" statements
- [ ] No "100% accurate" statements
- [ ] No "direct ERP integration" without customer schema verification
- [ ] No manufacturer/supplier logos
- [ ] No fake testimonials or usage numbers
- [ ] No savings claims without verified data

## Required Disclaimer

"Automated extraction and consistency checks support data preparation. The customer must review flagged and business-critical records before ERP import, RFQ issuance, purchasing, maintenance, or engineering use."
