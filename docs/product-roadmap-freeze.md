---

## Freeze period

30 days after launch readiness.

Start counting when Tier-1 URLs are indexed, conversion events are firing, and the first traffic campaign is live. Do not extend the freeze because of new feature ideas — extend only if a production blocker prevents measurement.

---

## Why freeze

SectorCalc has enough product surface for the first traffic and revenue test:

- 100 free calculators
- 27 premium analyzers
- premium report preview/export
- free-to-premium CTA
- SEO authority architecture
- campaign and conversion tracking

Adding more product before measuring conversion increases complexity and delays revenue learning.

---

## Allowed work

- production blocker fixes
- SEO indexing blockers
- mobile usability blockers
- broken route/link fixes
- premium gate/export leaks
- CTA microcopy based on data
- pricing clarity fixes based on data
- beta partner flow fixes
- Search Console issue fixes

See [Week-1 Fix Rules](./week-1-fix-rules.md) and [Revenue Pricing Friction Rules](./revenue-pricing-friction-rules.md) for data-driven fix criteria.

---

## Not allowed

- new calculators
- new premium schemas
- new sectors
- homepage redesign
- pricing model redesign
- new dashboard
- admin expansion
- new AI features
- new payment architecture
- broad refactor

If a request falls outside allowed work, route it through [Backlog Intake](./backlog-intake-template.md).

---

## Backlog rule

Every new idea is added to backlog with:

- expected revenue impact
- affected page/tool
- supporting data
- risk
- effort
- decision date

No backlog item enters the sprint without measured data. See [Monetization Decision Gates](./monetization-decision-gates.md).
